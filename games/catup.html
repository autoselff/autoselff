About

CatUp is an engaging platform arcade game where you play the role of a mother cat searching for her five missing kittens. Your epic journey takes you through four diverse biomes: vast and perilous caves, plains, hills, and mysterious floating islands. To overcome the obstacles you encounter, you'll need to showcase exceptional gaming skills.

During this adventure, you'll have the opportunity to collect valuable items, such as valuable diamonds that can help you uncover hidden treasures and secrets. Besides diamonds, you'll find secret passages that allow you to complete the game more quickly and efficiently, adding an extra layer of strategy to your journey.

The entire adventure in CatUp is enriched with a self-composed soundtrack. Immerse yourself in the fascinating journey of a mother cat, explore unknown lands, collect valuable diamonds, and find your missing kittens, experiencing incredible adventures in CatUp
